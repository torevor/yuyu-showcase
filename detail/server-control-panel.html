Server Control Panel

One dashboard that starts and stops the heavy laptop apps, with a concurrency budget that refuses to overload the machine.

▶ Open it
▸ ops · Runs on the laptop (ps14), binds to the Tailscale interface — the tailnet is the trust boundary. The link works only when you are on the tailnet and the launcher is up.

A single dashboard that starts and stops the heavy laptop apps — GDELT Discourse Live, Fulcrum — with live per-step progress and streaming logs. Its load-bearing trick is a concurrency budget that refuses to launch two resource-hungry apps at once, so the laptop never falls over mid-demo. On boot it reconciles reality, adopting whatever is already running rather than falsely reporting it stopped.
