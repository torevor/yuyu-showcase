▶ Open itHow it works →

What it does

Live GDELT stream

Every 15 minutes it streams what the world's media just wrote into a fast analytics database, roughly 120,000+ articles a day.

Rich article tagging

Each article is tagged with themes, named entities, a six-axis emotional tone, and ~2,300 affect dimensions.

Plain-English querying

Ask a question in plain English and it writes a safe database query in front of you, returning fresh headlines within minutes of publication.

Planetary mood map

It paints the planet's mood as a clay-to-teal spectrum.

▸ ops · The ClickHouse + Postgres stack is always-on, but the public gdelt.elua2.org address is a Cloudflare tunnel — on ps14 run gdelt-tunnel once per boot and keep the window open.

The global news firehose, live. Every 15 minutes GDELT publishes what the world's media just wrote, and this app streams that into a fast analytics database — roughly 120,000+ articles a day. Each is tagged with themes, named entities, a six-axis emotional tone, and ~2,300 affect dimensions.

Ask a question in plain English and it writes a safe database query in front of you, returns fresh headlines within minutes of publication, and paints the planet's mood as a clay-to-teal spectrum.
