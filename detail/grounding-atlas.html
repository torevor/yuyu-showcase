Grounding Atlas

Every definition is made of words you also have to look up — this maps the loops and finds the words underneath them all

▶ Open it

What it does

Definition circularity map

Maps how dictionary definitions refer to other words that themselves need looking up, across roughly 500 real definitions.

Loop detection

Finds the circular loops where words are ultimately defined in terms of each other.

Primitive word isolation

Isolates the small set of primitive words that everything else is ultimately defined in terms of.

If you look up a word, its definition uses other words — which you also have to look up. Grounding Atlas maps that circularity across ~500 real dictionary definitions.

It finds the loops in that web and isolates the small set of primitive words everything else is ultimately defined in terms of.
